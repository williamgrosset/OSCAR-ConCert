Beginning in 2002, we will be offering a new and improved weekend service. All of the doctors in our three units have formed a weekend on-call group. Patients can telephone us directly on the weekend and arrange to be seen by the on-call doctor.
How will the new system work?
If you have a medical problem on the weekend, dial the number for your own doctor as usual. If you need to be seen by a doctor, the receptionist will arrange a time for you to come to the clinic that day. If you call in the evening you will be directed to the usual on-call service.
What are the benefits to patients?
� Increased direct access to a doctor on weekends
� Convenient, central Weekend Clinic location
� Acute services available near the Weekend Clinic
� Lots of street parking
� Wheelchair accessible
� Your family doctor will be notified about your visit before the office opens Monday
What about Residents?
Family practice residents will take turns working under supervision at the Weekend On-Call Service.
